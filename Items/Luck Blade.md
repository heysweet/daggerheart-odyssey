*Weapon (any sword), legendary (requires attunement)*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While the sword is on your person, you also gain a +1 bonus to saving throws.

_**Luck.**_ Once per session, if the sword is on your person, you can call on its luck (no action required) to reroll one attack roll, ability check, or saving throw you dislike. You must use the second roll.

_**Wish.**_ The sword has 1d4–1 charges. Once per session while holding it, you can use an action to expend 1 charge and cast the [wish](https://www.dndbeyond.com/spells/2303-wish) spell from it. The sword loses this property if it has no charges.

OR IF WISH IS NOT GOOD FOR DAGGERHEART

_**Adjust Reality..**_ The sword has 4 charges. Once per session, at no cost other than 1 charge, after you or a willing ally makes any kind of roll, be it damage, attack, or action, you can change the result of the roll to any other number so long as it's realistic.
