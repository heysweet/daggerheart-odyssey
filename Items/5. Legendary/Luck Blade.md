*Weapon (any sword), legendary (requires attunement)*

You gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic weapon. While the sword is on your person, you also gain a +1 bonus to saving throws.

_**Luck.**_ Once per session, if the sword is on your person, you can call on its luck (no action required) to reroll one attack roll, ability check, or saving throw you dislike. You must use the second roll.

_**Alter Divine Decree.**_ This item has 3 charges. Once per session, you may **Spend 3 Hope** to alter an existing Oath or undo a Divine Curse.
